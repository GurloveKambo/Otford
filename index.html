The Estate

Where the Ocean
Meets Luxury

One of only a handful of true prestige properties along the Northern Illawarra coastline, Infinity at Otford commands an elevated position that delivers both absolute privacy and breathtaking 200° views extending along the coast and out over the Pacific.

Every morning brings a new horizon — watch the sun rise over the ocean from the comfort of your bed, spot migrating whales offshore, or follow dolphins from the deck. Photos cannot do justice to the extraordinary outlook this property commands.

Set on 9,718m² perched high on the hilltop, surrounded by native bush and overlooking ocean and valley below, this is the ultimate coastal escape — within an hour of Sydney, yet worlds away from it.

Master Suite
Occupying the entire top floor: an immense main bedroom, a walk-in robe of equal proportions, a lavish ensuite — and panoramic ocean views the moment you open your eyes.
25m Infinity Pool & Spa
Engineered into the deck with the horizon as its edge, the infinity pool is both an architectural feat and an unforgettable experience — the ideal setting for long summer evenings.
Gourmet Kitchen
A chef's dream featuring a spacious walk-in pantry, island bench with breakfast bar, and views that make every meal an occasion.
The Gallery Garage
250m² of concrete-floored showroom for up to 10 vehicles — as much gallery as garage — alongside ample storage and a stable on the grounds.

Every Season
Has Its Magic

Long Days,
Endless Pool
December through February delivers the best of coastal living — warm ocean breezes, long golden evenings by the infinity pool, and the Pacific at its most inviting. The 25m pool becomes the centrepiece of every day.

March to May brings mild temperatures, thinner crowds and extraordinary quality of light over the ocean. One of the best times for hiking and photography, with the landscape alive in colour.
